FAQ's

What is Kaitiakitanga?

Kaitiakitanga, in Maori, refers to the idea of being guardians and protectors of our environment. This is shown through sustainable practices and having a deep respect for nature.

What is Manaakitanga?

Manaakitanga, on the other hand, means to show care and compassion to one another. Like how we should care for eachother in our efforts to protect our environment and it's treasures.

Why Project Kaitiaki?

Our native wildlife are now facing serious challenges, and without the support of initiatives like Project Kaitiaki, we risk losing them forever. By understanding Kaitiakitanga and Manaakitanga, along with taking action, you can make a real difference in preserving New Zealand's natural beauty for generations to come. Join us in this vital mission.
